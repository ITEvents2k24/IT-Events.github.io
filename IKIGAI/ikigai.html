ABOUT EVENT: IKIGAI

This exciting event features three rounds with teams of 3 members:

ROUND 1:

RHYTHM RIDDLE: In this fun and engaging round, participants will listen to short clips of songs and try to guess the lyrics. The quicker and more accurate they are, the more points they earn. This round will test their musical memory and lyrical knowledge.

ROUND 2:

BRIDGE BUILDERS: In this round, participants will be given a set of clues or content that could represent the name of a song, movie, proverb, idiom, or other concepts. By analyzing these clues, they need to determine the correct answer. This fun filled round tests their general knowledge and ability to think creatively under pressure.

ROUND 3:

VISHUAL VOYAGE: Participants will be shown various logos of popular brands or companies. They will need to correctly identify and name the brand or organization represented by the logo. This round will challenge their visual recognition and brand knowledge.
